I am a freelance writer with bylines in The Guardian, The Independent, the i, the Metro and the Huffington Post. I write non-fiction & fiction about a variety of topics, including:

  • surrogacy
  • mental health
  • sexuality and gender
  • the environment

Background

I grew up in West Yorkshire and then went to Warwick University to study Discrete Mathematics. After university, I moved to Oxford, where I have lived for the last decade. Since 2013, I've worked as a Java software developer and I started freelance writing in 2019. On the personal side, I am an avid aerial hoop amateur and I recently carried a child as an altruistic surrogate. My pronouns are they/them.
